## v0.6 Change

v0.6 adds a **Columbia failure-pair layer** to the Apollo 13 expression layer.

Apollo 13 remains the positive case: correction reaches the affected subjects before hardening. Columbia is the discriminating failure pair: signal and partial process existed, but uncertainty, authority, communication, and correction did not remain linked tightly enough to alter the path before re-entry hardened the loss.

This artifact still does **not** validate TRACE or Mechanical Ethics. The pair is a structured illustration of discrimination: the same operators should distinguish a successful correction network from a failed one. That is stronger than a single positive case, but it is still not empirical validation.

## Use Contract

Use this concordance to map ME/TRACE operators onto established disciplines and to see where cases express those operators.

Do **not** use Apollo or Columbia to claim ME is proven. They are hand-selected cases. Their value is that they make the operator differences inspectable:

- Apollo 13: correction network bites in time.
- Columbia: correction network exists in principle but fails to bite before hardening.

## Immediate Reading

The Apollo/Columbia pair shows why the Concordance is more valuable than another film or history essay. The reusable object is not the story. The reusable object is the operator map.

Apollo 13 expresses the keeper positively: correction beats hardening because witness, record, authority, time, enforcement, repair, and distributed expertise remain coupled.

Columbia expresses the failure mode: signal and analysis exist, but management assumptions, weak challenge routes, uncertainty closure, and insufficient consequence-carrier prevent correction from becoming binding before the hardening event.

The strongest ME/TRACE remainder is still narrow: correction is clocked and conjunctive. It must reach the affected path in time, and every K-factor must remain live enough to carry correction through.

## Boundaries

- This is a concordance and case-expression artifact, not validation.
- The Columbia layer does not claim that rescue was simple or certain.
- The point is narrower: a correction network can fail before repair is even knowable if signal, authority, and uncertainty handling do not remain live.
- No new bootstrap is required yet. A full Columbia bootstrap may be useful later, but only if it adds discriminating structure beyond this table.
