## Purpose

This folder maps TRACE into the substrate of living systems: boundary, energy, repair, dependency, ecology, predation, symbiosis, ageing, and death.

It exists because TRACE cannot only map institutions, AI, policy, and human stories. Those structures sit on top of life.

