## Prior single-anchor read

Maisie was mapped in the Positive TRACE Atlas as:

```trace
Maisie_positive_read :=
  nonhuman_subject_signal
  + care_without_contract
  + dependency_increases_responsibility
  + maintenance_love
  + survival_with_scar
  + dignity_under_impairment
  + temperament_continuity
  + end_of_life_humility
```

That is useful, but insufficient alone. A sentimental read could preserve love while under-reading biological welfare truth. A biological read could preserve welfare truth while flattening relation. An agency read could over-project human agency into nonverbal signals.

```trace
single_atlas_risks :=
  sentimental_love_without_welfare_truth
  OR biology_without_relation
  OR agency_projection_without_signal
```
