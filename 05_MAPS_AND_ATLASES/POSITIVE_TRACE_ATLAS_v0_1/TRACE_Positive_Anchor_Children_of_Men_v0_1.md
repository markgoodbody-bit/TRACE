## Why Children of Men belongs here

The Positive TRACE Atlas has moved through home, emotion, hostile systems, planetary abandonment, moral repair, hospitality, and nonhuman care.

Children of Men adds civilisation-scale future collapse.

```trace
Children_of_Men :=
  no_children_world
  + future_space_collapse
  + state_hardening
  + refugee_disposability
  + single_birth_as_future_signal
  + escort_under_fire
```

Plain version: the film asks what remains of ethics when the future has disappeared. It is not just dystopia. It is a positive anchor because it shows why one vulnerable future-bearing subject can reorganise meaning, risk, courage, and protection.

## Birth interrupts violence

## Hope

Hope in Children of Men is not optimism.

```trace
hope :=
  future_not_empty
  + someone_to_protect
  + route_still_possible
  + cost_accepted
```

The film refuses cheap hope. There is no guarantee that the Human Project will save the world. But there is enough possibility to justify action.

```trace
thin_hope :=
  low_certainty
  + high_stakes
  + nonzero_future_path
  -> act
```

This is one of the best TRACE hope definitions in the atlas.
