## Compact table

| Primitive | Anchor pressure | Positive function | Main failure / demoter |
|---|---|---|---|
| `play_as_safe_simulation` | Bluey | Lets a child practise agency, rules, emotion, and power in a reversible world. | Fails when play becomes domination, humiliation, adult capture, or no-reality-tether fantasy. |
| `home_as_repair_habitat` | Bluey | Makes error survivable and correction reachable without exile. | Fails when every error becomes identity, child signals are ignored, or adult power never bends down. |
| `child_time` | Bluey | Protects slow, repetitive, inefficient meaning-formation. | Fails when optimisation, hurry, or adult convenience consumes developmental rhythm. |
| `correction_without_exile` | Bluey / Paddington | Names wrongdoing or mismatch while keeping the subject inside the protected circle. | Fails as either no correction or correction that expels/humiliates. |
| `sadness_as_repair_signal` | Inside Out | Lets loss reach the centre so help, grief, and reclassification become possible. | Fails when sadness is suppressed as defect or treated as harm by itself. |
| `mixed_memory_as_maturity` | Inside Out | Allows joy, grief, love, and loss to coexist without identity collapse. | Fails under purity-demand: only happy memory or only bad memory allowed. |
| `single_signal_capture` | Inside Out / The Good Place / WALL-E | Detects when one good value takes over total governance. | Fails when happiness, comfort, security, score, or future is allowed to override all other signals. |
| `name_as_identity_key` | Spirited Away | Preserves self-access, memory, relation, and return-path under capture pressure. | Fails when a subject is reduced to role, case number, asset, debtor, diagnosis, or symbol. |
| `pollution_not_identity` | Spirited Away | Separates damaged presentation from subject nature. | Fails when contamination, trauma, poverty, illness, or stigma is treated as the subject itself. |
| `false_gold` | Spirited Away | Detects symbolic value that hacks attention while real need remains unseen. | Fails when money, status, metrics, applause, or reward tokens replace subject-recognition. |
| `slow_repair` | Spirited Away | Allows removal from a corrupt accelerator and time for integration. | Fails when closure is forced before the aftereffect can be metabolised. |

| `comfort_capture` | WALL-E | Detects agency erosion through over-comfort, friction removal, automation, and distraction. | Fails when absence of pain is mistaken for absence of harm. |

| `ending_as_release` | The Good Place | Allows completion, consent, and non-coercive exit as not necessarily harm. | Fails when all endings are treated as failure or retention is forced as goodness. |
| `hospitality_as_repair_habitat` | Paddington | Receives vulnerable strangers with shelter, boundary, recognition, and updating. | Fails as naive welcome, exploitative welcome, or threat-label before subject-recognition. |

| `mistake_not_malice` | Paddington | Distinguishes disruption from predatory intent where learning remains possible. | Fails when all bad outcomes become character condemnation. |

## Demoter questions

Use these before promoting any positive reading.

```trace
demoter_questions :=
  is_this_mapping_sentimental?
  + did_TRACE_add_structure_or_only_rename_the_obvious?
  + did_the_read_preserve_the_felt_life_of_the_case?
  + did_it_make_any_harm_or_cost_invisible?
  + did_it turn a subject into a symbol?
  + did_it mistake beginning_for_repair?
  + did_it erase accountability?
```

Plain version:

A positive TRACE read fails if it becomes syrup. It also fails if it becomes cold mechanism. The goal is structure without killing the life of the case.
