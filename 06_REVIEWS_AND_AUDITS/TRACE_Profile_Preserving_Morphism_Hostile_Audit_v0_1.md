## 2. Main overclaim risk: `morphism`

The word `morphism` carries mathematical weight. In category theory and algebra, a morphism usually has defined objects, structure, and composition rules. This file has a candidate translation map, not a proven morphism in a formal category.

```trace
morphism_overclaim_risk :=
  word_morphism
  may_be_read_as:
    category_theory_completed
    OR structure_preserving_map_formally_proven
    OR composition_laws_defined
```

Patch required:

```trace
patch_morphism_status :=
  morphism := candidate_translation_map_shape
  not:
    formal_category_theoretic_morphism
    OR theorem
    OR proof
```
