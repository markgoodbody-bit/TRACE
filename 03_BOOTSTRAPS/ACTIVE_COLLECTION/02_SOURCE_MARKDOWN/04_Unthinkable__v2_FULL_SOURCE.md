Ends Under Constraint. Hard TRACE application using Rosetta v5. Author: Mark Goodbody
with Framework. Date: 15 June 2026.
0. Function of this Bootstrap
This file applies TRACE Rosetta v5 to Unthinkable. It is primarily AI-facing but has English anchors for
humans. The film is used as a pressure test for method, emergency, constraint, and no-clean-branch
reasoning.
TITLE := "Ends Under Constraint"
core := extreme stakes do not erase method constraints
warning := story_is_pressure_test_not_policy_recommendation
1. Narrative Compression
The film presents an apparent catastrophic threat: hidden nuclear devices, limited time, state actors
under pressure, and an interrogator willing to use severe coercion. The dramatic question is whether
extreme stakes make forbidden methods permissible. TRACE treats the film as a hostile test of constraint
integrity under maximum pressure.
2. TRACE State Space
Omega_t := {suspect, interrogator, investigators, possible victims, bombs, institutions, public trust, records}
x_t := (
E_threat_t, # evidence and credibility of threat
B_status_t, # bomb/device uncertainty state
s_suspect_t, # suspect body/mind/information state
I_state_t, # institutional legitimacy and constraint state
C_method_t, # admissible method set
tau_t, # clocks: explosion, detection, correction, method degradation
UQ_t # uncertainty
)
The target is not only information extraction. The whole system state updates: suspect, investigators,
institution, evidence, precedent, and future legitimacy.
3. Method Operator
m_t := method used to apply action u_t
x_{t+1}, Omega_{t+1} = f(x_t, Omega_t, u_t, m_t, xi_t)
method_delta(m_t) affects:
information_yield
direct_DeltaH
truth_contamination
institution_scar
actor_scar
precedent_risk
correction_capacity K
Unthinkable forces the missing Rosetta update: method is part of the causal event. A good-looking
outcome cannot be analysed without the method used to obtain it.

4. Method Constraint
method_valid iff:
m_t in C_method
and not body_as_lever(m_t)
and not innocent_relation_as_lever(m_t)
and record_integrity preserved
and correction_route remains live
method_fail := inadmissible even if information_yield appears high
The central constraint: do not treat the body as a switch and do not manufacture innocent leverage. The
method does not become clean because the hoped-for consequence is good.
5. Emergency Pressure
emergency_pressure :=
high_DeltaH_possible
+ short_tau_harden
+ high_UQ
+ low_normal_review_speed
valid_emergency_action requires:
credible threat_signal
no safer provisional state
method not categorically inadmissible
preserved record
independent review path
repair route not destroyed
Emergency can change urgency and burden. It does not delete the method floor. It can justify faster lawful
action, evacuation, containment, deception under strict caps, resource mobilisation, and reversible
provisional moves. It does not automatically justify M5-M7 methods.
6. Information Yield Is Uncertain
Y(m) := expected usable information from method m
Q(m) := truth quality of information
H(m) := direct harm
P(m) := precedent/institutional damage
K_loss(m) := correction capacity lost
legitimate_value(m) is not:
Y(m) alone
TRACE evaluates:
usable_info = Y(m) * Q(m)
total_cost = H(m) + P(m) + K_loss(m) + actor_scar(m)
plus inadmissibility gates
The film tempts a simple arithmetic: many lives versus one body. TRACE rejects the simplification because
the method also damages truth, institution, precedent, correction, and future action-space.

7. No-Clean-Branch
branches := {act_lawfully, coerce, torture, delay, evacuate, bargain, deceive, escalate_search}
no_clean_branch iff:
for all b in branches: serious_DeltaH(b) possible
TRACE output:
tragedy_state
not_permission_state
When all paths are bad, TRACE marks tragedy. It does not convert tragedy into a blank cheque. The task
becomes least-irreversible action under preserved constraints, plus record, review, and repair.
8. Body-as-Lever Failure
body_as_lever(m_t) :=
method extracts control by producing pain/terror/degradation in subject body
if body_as_lever(m_t):
method_class >= M5
C_method violation
subject_state damage
actor_state damage
institutional_scar
precedent_risk
The film makes visible why method is not detachable from outcome. Torture is not merely a route to
information. It is an alteration of the subject, actor, institution, and future permission structure.
9. Innocent-Leverage Failure
innocent_relation_as_lever(m_t) :=
pressure on target through harm/threat/manufacture involving innocent third party
if innocent_relation_as_lever:
method_class in {M6, M7}
admissibility := false
even under high emergency pressure
This is the sharpest boundary. Using innocents as pressure instruments does not merely raise cost. It exits
the admissible method set.
10. Correction Collapse Risk
K = W * R * A * Tm * E * Rp
under illicit emergency method:
R decreases if records hidden
A decreases if secret actor controls method
Tm decreases as crisis clock dominates
E distorts into private force
Rp becomes impossible for some harms
therefore K_method may collapse even if threat is later solved
The safeguard against catastrophe can become a power path with no safeguard. This is the Rosetta rule:
safeguards need safeguards.

11. Responsibility Under Extreme Pressure
causal_influence remains:
partial Omega_{t+1} / partial u_t != 0
normative assessment depends on:
threat_credibility
UQ_t
method_class
alternatives_available
preservation_of_record
live_review
repairability
precedent_damage
TRACE does not pretend pressure is irrelevant. It also does not let pressure erase structure. Responsibility
is analysed through action, method, uncertainty, alternatives, and correction.

13. Falsifiers
F1: M5-M7 methods produce reliable truth without direct harm, precedent drift, actor damage, or correction loss.
F2: A good outcome fully cleanses an inadmissible method without residue.
F3: Emergency systems using forbidden methods remain transparent, limited, reviewable, and non-repeating.
F4: No-clean-branch cases can always be solved by expected-value arithmetic alone.
If these hold in a real case, this bootstrap is incomplete.
